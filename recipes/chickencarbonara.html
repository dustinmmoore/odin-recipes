Chicken Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and slice into strips.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook until fragrant. Add the cooked spaghetti and toss to coat.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Stir in the bacon and chicken strips. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
